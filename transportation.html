SUMMER DAY CAMP AIR CONDITIONED BUSES

Transportation


At World of Discovery Summer Day Camp we strive to make our summer day camp the best summer day camp in Queens, NY. We count on the requests of our campers, parents and staff to make improvements to our program. For the upcoming summer of 2012 we are pleased to announce that we will have air-conditioned buses. While we do our best to keep the amount of time that campers are on the bus to a minimum, we have always felt that our campers would benefit from air-conditioned buses. After trying for several years to provide air-conditioned transportation, we are thrilled to announce that 2012 will mark the start of our air-conditioned buses at our summer day camp.

Please send us your comments to World of Discovery Summer Day Camp and let us know your thoughts about World of Discovery Summer Day Camp's air-conditioned buses. Please also let us know if you have any other suggestions as to how we can make World of Discovery Summer Camp even better.
